Ingedients

  • Flour

  • Garlic Salt

  • Paprika

  • Pepper

  • Poultry Seasoning

  • Chicken

  • Salt

  • Eggs

  • Oil

Recipe

  1. In a large shallow dish, combine 2-2/3 cups flour, garlic salt, paprika 2-1/2 teaspoons pepper and poultry seasoning.

  2. In another shallow dish, beats eggs and 1-1/2 cups water; add salt and the remaining 1-1/3 cups of flour and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.

  3. Dip chicken in egg mixture, then place in flour mixture, a few pieces at a time. Turn to coat.

  4. In a deep-fat fryer, heat oil to 375 degrees.

  5. Fry chicken, several pieces at a time, until chicken is golden brown and juices run clear 7-8 minutes on each side. Drain on paper towels.

  6. Use neutral oils such as canola or vegetable oil.

  • Soy Sauce

  • Garlic

  • Ginger

  • Red Chili Flakes

Recipe

  1. Use boneless skinless chicken breast or thighs.

  2. Cut into bite-size piece. Dredge chicken in whisked eggs and cornstarch/flour mixture until fully coated and get oil ready.

  3. To make sauce, use orange juice, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and red chili flakes in a small pot and cook over medium-high heat. Add cornstarch and water and cook until thickened.

  4. Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot over medium-high heat. When frying foods, use thermometer. Let the oil heat to 350 degrees. Once oil is ready, in batches fry chicken for about 2 minutes until light golden brown. Repeat.

  5. Toss chicen in sauce and serve.
